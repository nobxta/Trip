Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the trip?

Joining the trip is simple! Just follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Visit our website and go to the registration page.
  • Step 2: Fill up the required details, including your name, contact info, and preferred trip.
  • Step 3: Choose your payment option and complete the payment.
  • Step 4: Once your payment is confirmed, you’re all set! You will receive a confirmation email and be added to the WhatsApp group for trip updates.
That’s it! Now just pack your bags and get ready for an amazing experience. 🚀

What if I can’t book right now because I don’t have enough money?

No worries! You can still secure your spot by paying a Seat Lock Fee. This allows you to temporarily reserve your seat while you arrange the remaining amount.

You will need to pay the full trip fee at least 10 days before the trip to confirm your participation. If you do not complete the payment within this period, your seat will be given to another participant.

What should I bring with me?

You are free to take whatever you need for the trip, but some essentials include:

  • Cash: Carry enough money depending on the activities you wish to do at the destination.
  • Government-issued ID: Aadhar card, passport, or driver's license is mandatory for verification.
  • Medicines: Basic medicines for headache, fever, or body pain.
  • Power Bank: Keep your phone charged during travel.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Pack as per the weather and activities planned.

How will I receive trip updates and notifications?

After registering for the trip via our website, you will receive all updates and notifications via email and WhatsApp. We will add all participants to a dedicated WhatsApp group where important announcements, schedule updates, and reminders will be shared.
